Octopus Energy Tariffs Explained

A referral link should not be the only reason you switch. Octopus has standard, fixed, and smart options, so look at how you actually use power before choosing a tariff.

Tariff or setup Who it may suit
Flexible Octopus Homes that want a variable tariff with no fixed-contract commitment.
Fixed Octopus tariffs Homes that want more predictable prices for a set period.
Agile Octopus Smart-meter homes that can shift electricity use away from expensive periods.
Octopus Tracker Customers comfortable with rates that follow wholesale energy pricing.
Octopus Go or Intelligent Octopus Go EV drivers who charge at home and can benefit from cheaper overnight electricity.
Cosy Octopus Homes with heat pumps that can use cheaper electricity windows.

Before switching, double-check the current rates, any exit fees with your existing supplier, and whether a smart meter is required for the tariff you want. Referral credit can be useful, but it should not outweigh a worse tariff or unsuitable terms.
