EV Charger FAQ

Here are a few quick things to think about before you order. For all the technical details, compatibility checks, and full FAQs, make sure to read the official Octopus EV charger page.

Tethered or untethered?

Tethered chargers have a cable permanently fixed to the unit. Untethered chargers let you plug your own cable in at both ends—perfect if you want a tidier look or prefer to use the cable that came with your EV.

Will the cable reach?

Your charging cable needs to comfortably reach from the chargepoint to your car. According to Octopus, tethered chargers typically come with cables between 5 and 8 metres long, which is plenty for most situations where the charger is mounted on an outside wall next to a driveway.

Do you need private parking?

Yes. Octopus requires you to have private parking like a driveway or garage, with no public land separating your house from the charger. If your cable needs to run across public land, you'll generally need a different solution, like Kerbo Charge.
