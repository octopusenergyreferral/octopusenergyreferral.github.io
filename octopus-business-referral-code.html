Octopus Energy is a UK energy company that offers business tariffs with competitive rates. There are various options available, including variable, fixed, and green tariffs.

To switch to Octopus Energy, you'll need to give notice to your current provider and Octopus Energy will handle the rest of the process.

Currently, you can earn a £75 referral reward from Octopus Energy, plus my £35 reward, by switching either gas, electricity, or both fuels to Octopus Energy using the provided referral link and submitting your business's post code. That makes the total offer worth £110.

  • How do I track my energy usage and costs with Octopus Energy?

    Octopus Energy provides access to an online account portal where you can view your energy usage and costs in real-time.

    You can also download detailed usage reports to help you monitor and manage your energy consumption.

  • Can I set up a direct debit for my business energy bills with Octopus Energy?

    Octopus Energy offers the option to set up a direct debit for your business energy bills.

    This can make it easier to manage your bills.

  • Does Octopus Energy offer any incentives for businesses that use zero-carbon energy sources?

    Yes, Octopus Energy offers a range of tariffs that are specifically designed for businesses that use zero-carbon energy sources.

    These tariffs often come with additional benefits, such as reduced rates or credits on your energy bill.

  • Is it possible to switch to Octopus Energy if my business is located outside of the UK?

    If you're located outside of the UK and are interested in switching to a zero-carbon energy provider, you may want to consider contacting companies that serve your region.
